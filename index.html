Welcome


1 in 6 people worldwide experience a disability. Targeting this population is our mission. Starting in 2018, we began supporting all service seekers, persons with disabilities, their parents and caregivers, schools, and educators in the neighborhood. We partner with day care centers, schools, pediatric clinics, municipalities, and other educational institutions to maximize our outreach. By networking with all similar centers in the neighborhood, we either provide people with necessary services or refer them to the appropriate service provider.

Why Choose TCTW?

TCTW is managed by professionals in special education services who are dedicated to providing inclusive settings for their students. The services provided by TCTW are imperative, vital, and second to none in our circumference. By choosing TCTW, you can trust that your child will receive the highest quality of special education services in an environment that promotes inclusivity and success.

Promoting Inclusion


Collaborating with parents on seeking best placement for their children is essential to promote their social and academic inclusion. As well, planning with persons with disabilities themselves to seek better vocational training, internship and career building promotes their inclusion and independency.

Supporting parents/caregivers

Empowering parents/caregivers through supporting and providing them with strategies through coaching, guidance and parenting sessions enables the parents to become more resilient.

Integrity


Respecting persons with disabilities and their families and treating them honestly is one of our core values.
