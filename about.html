About Us

“Promote, protect and ensure full and equal enjoyment of all human rights by all persons with disabilities, and to promote respect for their inherent dignity” CRPD


As a result of our belief in the above-mentioned, TCTW was founded in 2018, registered under 798 AD in August 2021 to serve as the first of its kind in the area. Till now, TCTW supported more than 150 children and their families through the provision of 1,350 therapeutical, consultation, guidance, mentoring and follow-up sessions. Besides TCTW is a pioneer in the area empowering schools in their shifts to inclusion.

Our Services




Services


At TCTW Lebanon, we provide people of different age groups with a wide range variety of services. We cater for almost all difficulties and disabilities because our team is well experienced, passionate and considerate. For better implementation and results, we collaborate with the educational institutions and parents/caregivers to form a team that works on the objectives set for a person with disability or difficulty.

  • Speech and Language Therapy
  • Psychomotor Therapy
  • Physiotherapy
  • Special Education
  • Psychological Therapy
  • Education Consultancy




Referal Services


In collaboration and networking with service providers in Aley district and neighboring regions, we refer beneficiaries to the suitable service provider if the service isn’t available at our center.




Education Support Services


Special educators at TCTW Lebanon offer individualized educational programs for children upon their needs in coordination with the educational institution.
